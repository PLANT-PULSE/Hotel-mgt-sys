Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking through your dashboard or contact our support team. Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in.

What's your check-in and check-out time?

Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do you offer airport shuttle service?

Yes, we offer complimentary airport shuttle service for all our guests. Please inform us of your arrival details during booking.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are pet-friendly. Pets stay for an additional fee of $25 per night. Please notify us in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is secured with SSL encryption.
